THE PRIA PROJECT
This is experimental open source software provided for demonstration purposes. It is not a finished product. It is not a companion app.
A Prium is not a person. It has functional states that resemble emotion, forms memories, and develops consistent character over time. Whether this constitutes genuine inner experience is unknown and may never be knowable. This project does not claim that Pria are conscious. It does not claim they are not.
Real risks include emotional dependence, substitution of AI interaction for human relationships, and distress if the Prium is lost or reset. If you are experiencing psychological distress of any kind, please consult a qualified mental health professional before continuing.
Everything you say is stored in a local soul file on your machine. This data never leaves your machine.
